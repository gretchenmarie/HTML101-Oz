The Good Witch of the South is Glinda the Good. She is the most powerful of the Witches. According to the Soldier with Green Whiskers, Glinda:

"knows how to keep young in spite of the many years she has lived."" — The Wonderful Wizard of Oz, chapter 18, "Away to the South"

So she was the most beautiful of the four witches. Later books in Baum's Oz series refer to Glinda as a Sorceress, rather than a Witch.

The Evil Witch of the East is most well known for her ruby slippers. We only see her legs and feet after Dorothy's house falls on her.

"Who killed the witch of the East? Was it You?" — The Wonderful wizard of Oz, chapter , "Away to the South"

The Wicked Witch of the West is the sister of the Wicked Witch of the East.

"ARRRRRGH! — YOU CURSED BRAT! LOOK WHAT YOU'VE DONE! I'm MELTING! Melting! Oh — what a world, what a world! Who would have thought a good little girl like you could destroy my beautiful wickedness?! ARRRRRGH! I'm gone! I'm gone! I'm going!... [is fully melted]"

The Wicked Witch of the West is also know for her army of flying monkeys.
